# Who is the customer?

---
> A stakeholder is a person, group, or organization that is actively involved in a project, is affected by its process or outcome, or can influence its process or outcome. 
> Stakeholders can be internal or external to the project team and to the developing organization.
---

## Outside the Developing Organization
* Direct user
* Indirect user
* Acquirer (represent the commercial side of the project)
* Procurement staff (Does the software meet industry standards (ISO, GDPR, SOC2)? Is it legal and low-risk?)
* Legal staff (For specific industries (like Banking or Healthcare), they ensure the software meets strict government mandates (like HIPAA for medical data or PCI-DSS for credit cards).)
